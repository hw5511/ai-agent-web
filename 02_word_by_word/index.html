About Henri Matisse
Fauvism Movement
French Artist, 1869–1954
— About the Artist

Henri Matisse (1869–1954) was a French artist known for his vibrant use of color and bold, expressive shapes.

— Legacy

A leading figure of the Fauvism movement, his work broke away from traditional representation, using vivid hues to convey emotion rather than realism.

— 1869 – 1954

Color is the keyboard, the eyes are the harmonies, the soul is the piano with many strings.
