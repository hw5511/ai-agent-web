In ages before the memory of man, when the heavens burned with primordial fire, a creature was born from the collision of starlight and volcanic flame. The GRIFLAN — half ember, half eternal spirit — roamed the boundless skies of the Primordial Realm.

Its sacred horn channels the raw energy of the cosmos, weaving spells of unimaginable power. Where it gallops, reality bends. Where it breathes, embers birth new worlds into existence from the void.

Now the GRIFLAN awakens once more. The ancient prophecy unfolds. Those who bear its mark shall inherit the power of the eternal flame and shape the destiny of realms yet unseen.
