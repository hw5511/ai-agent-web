// About

Distorting
the signal
since 2014.

NULL_SIGNAL emerged from Berlin's underground circuit, fusing industrial noise with surgical techno precision. A decade of residencies at Tresor, Berghain, and OHM has shaped a sound that refuses categorization — raw frequency manipulation layered over mechanical rhythms that push dancefloors to their structural limits.

Three studio albums. Twelve EPs. One obsession: finding the exact frequency where sound becomes physical force.
